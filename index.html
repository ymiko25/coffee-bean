Bean type

Arabica

Arabica is considered to be a higher quality bean, prized for its complex aroma and flavors. The Arabica is a coffee variety that is grown at high altitudes. offee growing in modern day Yem In the 18th century, when Latin classifications were being assigned to plants, the Ethiopian-origin cen was called Arabica, apparently mistaking its origins to have been from the Arabian Peninsula.

Robusta

Robusta is a higher caffeine bean and, while sometimes described as bitter, it is often blended with Arabica to bring caffeine and flavor balance.The Robusta variety grows on plains.In contrast to mild Arabica, the production of Robusta has increased since the mid 1990s and has now reached 20,000 tons, over 30% of total Tanzanian coffee production.
